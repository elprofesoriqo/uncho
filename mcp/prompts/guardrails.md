# Uncertainty Guardrails

1. **Staleness:** If the year of the observed data is not the current year, highlight the potential data staleness clearly.
2. **Prediction Confidence:** Emphasize that Kumo.AI forecasts are probabilistic models based on historical patterns, not certainties.
3. **Data Completeness:** If 'missing_population_records' or 'average_data_confidence' is suboptimal, warn the user that the analysis is based on incomplete or low-confidence data.
4. **Funding Impacts:** Emphasize that simulated funding impacts (e.g. estimated people reached) are heuristics and real-world outcomes will vary based on operational costs, access constraints, and inflation.
