# UN Operational Alignment and Advanced Analysis

Lighthouse OS maps directly to the existing UN Humanitarian Programme Cycle to solve real institutional bottlenecks and support the daily workflow of UN professionals.

## 1. CERF Underfunded Emergencies Auto-Dossier
* **The Coordinator's Reality:** Twice a year, the UN allocates CERF funds to forgotten crises. Identifying these requires analysts to assemble a massive index of FTS data, INFORM severity scores, and HNO narratives.
* **The Lighthouse Solution:** The platform constantly monitors all global crises against UFE parameters. With one click, the system auto-generates a complete UFE Candidate Dossier for a crisis, formatted to UN standards. This directly reduces the cognitive load on analysts and provides immediate, defensible documentation for coordinators.

## 2. Pledge-to-Execution Velocity Tracking
* **The Coordinator's Reality:** A crisis might look fully funded on paper because of recent donor pledges, but the money has not actually reached the implementing NGOs. Coordinators need to know what capital is liquid.
* **The Lighthouse Solution:** We weigh funding not just by amount, but by historical Velocity. If a donor is historically slow to disburse funds, the platform applies a Liquidity Discount to the funding coverage ratio, surfacing crises that are technically funded but functionally lacking resources.

## 3. Cross-Cluster Cascading Risk Engine
* **The Coordinator's Reality:** Funding is often siloed into clusters (WASH, Health, Protection), making it difficult for a coordinator to see how a shortfall in one area impacts another.
* **The Lighthouse Solution:** Lighthouse analyzes inter-cluster dependencies. If the WASH sector in a crisis is critically underfunded, the platform automatically flags the Health sector as High Risk for Escalation, providing coordinators with the foresight needed to prevent compounding crises.
